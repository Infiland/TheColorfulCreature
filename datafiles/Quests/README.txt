Quests are loaded in quests.txt, they follow this format:

1. Title - The name of your quest
2. Task - Description/Task what the player has to do
3. Variable, the ingame variable that is followed
4. Amount - How much does the player need to complete in order to beat a quest
5. Reward - How much credits does the player get at the end of the quest
6. Weight - Rarity of the quest

EXAMPLE:

Level Beater|Beat 10 Levels|QUESTlevelcompleted|10|25|100

Title: Level Beater
Task: Beat 10 Levels
Variable: QUESTlevelcompleted
Amount: 10
Reward: 25
Weight: 100
